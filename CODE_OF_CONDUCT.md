# Code of Conduct

Open Edge AI is built by people with different backgrounds, goals, and levels of
experience. The project expects contributors and maintainers to keep discussion
professional, useful, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and considerate.
- Focus technical disagreement on facts, tradeoffs, and project goals.
- Give enough context for others to understand and reproduce issues.
- Respect maintainers' time by keeping discussions on topic.
- Credit ideas, research, and contributions when appropriate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, repeated off-topic disruption, or bad-faith argument.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Pressuring maintainers for unpaid private support.
- Any behavior that makes participation unsafe or hostile.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests;
temporarily limit participation; or ban participants from project spaces when
behavior harms the project or community.

If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers through the
project's GitHub moderation or security reporting channels. Include relevant
links, screenshots, and context when possible.
